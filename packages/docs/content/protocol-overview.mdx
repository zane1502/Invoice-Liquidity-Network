# Protocol Overview

The Invoice Liquidity Network (ILN) is a decentralised invoice factoring protocol that connects freelancers and SMEs with DeFi liquidity providers on the Stellar blockchain.

    Core Concepts
Invoice Tokenisation
Every invoice submitted to ILN is tokenised as a unique Soroban asset representing the right to receive payment. This enables:
Fractional ownership — Multiple LPs can fund a single invoice
Transferability — Invoice positions can be traded or collateralised
Programmability — Smart contract logic governs the entire lifecycle

Discount Rate Mechanism
The discount rate represents the yield earned by liquidity providers. It is determined by:
Market demand — Higher demand for funding = lower discount rates
Payer reputation — Verified payers command better rates
Invoice duration — Longer due dates = higher discounts

LP Yield = Invoice Amount × (Discount Rate / 10000)
